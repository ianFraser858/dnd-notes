# World of Alrunna (Bob Earth 2)

## Overview
A standard D&D fantasy setting with occasional inconsistencies. The campaign centers around the recent unsealing of a mind flayer civilization that had been locked away for a millennia.

---

## Locations

### The Ringed City
- **Type:** Major city
- **Significance:** Center of the mind flayer civilization
- **Status:** Recently unsealed after 1000 years
- **Features:**
  - Extensive sensor network for surveillance
  - Secret tunnels used by smugglers to exit the city
  - The Nightmares organization is known here

### Sledgehammer
- **Type:** City/Town
- **Notable Features:**
  - Ivan's smuggling operations based here
  - Ketamari Mander (library/archive where the party researched sensors)
- **Connections:** Smuggling route to Silverleaf (2-3 days travel)

### Silverleaf
- **Type:** City/Town
- **Notable Features:**
  - Ivan's cousin operates a warehouse here
- **Connections:** 2-3 day journey from Sledgehammer via smuggling caravan

## Factions

### The Nightmares
- **Type:** Organization
- **Status:** Known in the Ring City
- **Notes:** The Dreamcatchers operate under this larger organization

### The Dreamcatchers
- **Type:** Adventuring party (the PCs)
- **Members:** Moz, Ardryl, Jack BeQuick, Sierra, Zarius
- **Affiliation:** Under The Nightmares

### Mind Flayer Civilization
- **Type:** Aberration empire
- **Status:** Recently unsealed, presumably rebuilding/expanding
- **Center:** The Ringed City

### Smugglers Network
- **Type:** Criminal enterprise
- **Known Members:** Ivan (Sledgehammer), Ivan (Silverleaf)
- **Operations:** Moving goods between cities, avoiding mind flayer sensors

## Lore

### The Sealing
- Approximately 1000 years ago, the mind flayer civilization was sealed away
- The circumstances and method of sealing are currently unknown
- The recent unsealing is a major campaign event

### Mind Flayer Technology
Documented sensor types used throughout the Ringed City:
1. **Specialized Kin** - Mind flayer-related creatures serving as lookouts
2. **Illusion Tech** - Broadcasts images to remote viewing devices for city watch
3. **Beam Alarms** - Magical trip wires that trigger alerts
4. **Organic Sensors** - Beasts modified by mind flayers (small, Hanar-like with tentacles, or intellect devourer offshoots)
5. **Explosive Traps** - Magical explosives triggered by proximity
