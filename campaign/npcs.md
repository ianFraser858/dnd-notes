### Mind Flayers (Illithids)
- **Type:** Aberration civilization
- **Location:** The Ringed City (floating above Silvermoor)
- **Status:** Actively occupying Silvermoor (took control 2 weeks ago)
- **Territory:** All four city-states of Silvermoor (Silverleaf, Highgarden, Goldvine, Ivymont)
- **Technology/Forces:**
  - Sensors (various types for city surveillance):
    - Specialized mind flayer kin
    - Illusion-broadcasting tech monitored by city watch
    - Magical beam alarms (trip wires)
    - Organic sensors (small Hanar-like creatures, intellect devourer offshoots)
    - Potentially explosive traps
  - The Pyramid (mysterious structure in Silverleaf - people enter but don't return)
- **Control Methods:**
  - Local governments pledging fealty
  - Curfew enforcement (gates close at sundown, 10 PM curfew)
  - Curfew violators sent to the Pyramid
- **Notes:** "Squids" is considered a slur; proper term is Illithids or mind flayers
